# Identity Profile Extractor (IPE v2)

**Make any AI actually understand who you are.**

One guided conversation → a portable Context Profile you paste into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI platform. Your AI stops being generic and starts being *yours*.

**Created by [@metapromptjc](https://github.com/metapromptjc)**, builder of the Lyra prompt framework (15M+ views), a system for writing prompts that get better AI output. IPE is the next step: Lyra made your prompts better. IPE makes your AI understand *you*.

---

## The Problem

You're paying $20/month for an AI that forgets who you are every conversation. It doesn't know what you do, how you think, what you're building, or what drives you crazy about generic AI responses. So every interaction starts from zero and the output sounds like it was written for nobody in particular. You know AI can do more. It just doesn't know enough about you to do it.

## The Solution

IPE v2 builds a **Context Profile**. A structured document that captures who you are, how you think, how you work, and how you want AI to communicate with you. Including what it should **never** do.

Paste it into any AI platform. Every response is immediately better. No more repeating yourself. No more generic output.

> 🔒 **Privacy:** IPE runs entirely inside your AI conversation. Nothing is stored, transmitted, or collected. Your profile stays with you.

---

## How It Works

```
Step 1 → Paste IPE v2 into any AI (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini)
Step 2 → It gives you a prompt to extract what your current AI already knows about you
Step 3 → Paste that context back. IPE analyzes gaps and asks ~10 min of targeted questions  
Step 4 → Out comes your Context Profile. Paste it anywhere, forever
```

**No AI history?** Just say "Start fresh" and IPE builds your profile from scratch.

**Time investment:** 10-15 minutes once → permanently transformed AI experience.

## What's In Your Context Profile

| Section | What It Captures |
|---|---|
| **Who I Am** | Name, location, life stage, who you are in a sentence |
| **What I Do** | Role, business, industry, career arc |
| **Working On Now** | Active projects, priorities, key decisions ahead |
| **How I Think** | Processing style, decision-making, learning preferences |
| **How I Work** | Peak hours, energy patterns, tools, work rhythm |
| **How To Communicate With Me** | Format, tone, detail level, plus what to NEVER do |
| **What Drives Me** | Core values, motivations, definition of success |
| **Where I'm Going** | Short and long-term goals, growth areas |
| **Patterns To Know** | Blind spots, stress behaviors, strengths played to excess |
| **Constraints** | Current challenges, resource limits, sensitivities |

## Why "Never Do This" Matters

Most AI personalization tells the AI what TO do. IPE v2 also captures what your AI should **never** do:

- Specific words or phrases to avoid
- AI behaviors that annoy you (hedging, disclaimers, sycophantic filler)
- Formatting you hate
- Tone failures
- Interaction patterns to skip

This is the section most people never think to write, and it's the one that makes the biggest difference.
